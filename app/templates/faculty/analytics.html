Key Findings:

  • There is a strong positive correlation between study hours and predicted performance.
  • The distribution of predicted scores shows that most students are expected to perform in the 70-85 range.
Recommendations for Faculty:
  • Encourage students to maintain high attendance rates, as this has a significant impact on performance.
  • Provide additional support for students with lower previous grades, as they are at higher risk of underperforming.
  • Recommend effective study strategies to help students maximize the benefit of their study hours.
  • Consider subject-specific interventions based on the average predicted scores by subject.
